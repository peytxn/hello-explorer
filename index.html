Journey Through Space

Earth

Our Home Planet

Earth is the only planet we know of that’s bursting with life. From deep blue oceans and towering mountains to forests, deserts, and cities, Earth is always changing and full of surprises. It has just the right mix of air, water, and warmth to support plants, animals, and people. As Earth spins and orbits the Sun, it gives us day and night, seasons, and a place to call home—floating through space like a perfectly balanced spaceship.
